Workflow

From Request to Live Monitoring in Minutes

A secure, transparent enrollment flow that puts the device owner in control at every step.

1

Operator Creates Request

Operator generates a secure, time-limited consent link with tracking purpose, duration, and organisation details.

2

Owner Receives Link

Sent via WhatsApp, email, or QR code. Owner reviews the full consent agreement including what data is collected and why.

3

Owner Registers & Consents

Owner creates an account, registers their device, and explicitly grants consent. Credentials are securely hashed and stored.

4

Live Monitoring Begins

GPS tracking activates. Operator sees the device on the live map. Owner can pause or revoke consent at any time.

Security First

Enterprise-Grade Security at Every Layer

Built with cybersecurity as the foundation, not an afterthought.

JWT RBAC

Role-Based Access Control with separate JWT claims for operators and device owners. Routes are server-enforced.

Encrypted Vaults

All audit logs are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption with PBKDF2-derived keys before database storage.

Hashed Credentials

Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256. Plaintext credentials are never stored. Silent JWT refresh prevents re-login loops.

Consent Audit Log

Every consent action — grant, pause, resume, revoke — is timestamped, logged, and auditable by authorised operators.
